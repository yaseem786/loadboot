Dispatch Service Agreement

Ref LB-DSA-2026-004417 · Effective on Carrier acceptance
Between LoadBoot (“Dispatcher”) and M Yaseen Trucking LLC, USDOT 3456789 / MC 1234567, 1200 Trucker Way, Dallas, TX 75201 (“Carrier”). WHEREAS Dispatcher provides truck dispatch and back-office services; NOW, THEREFORE the parties agree to the following terms in full:
1. Definitions. “Dispatcher” means LoadBoot; “Carrier” the motor carrier below. “Booked through Dispatcher” means any Load Dispatcher sourced, negotiated, or issued/received the Rate Confirmation for, as recorded in LoadBoot. “Gross Line-Haul Revenue” is the base transportation charge, excluding fuel surcharge, lumper, detention, TONU, and accessorials unless expressly bundled.
2. Services. Dispatcher uses commercial best efforts to source freight, present options, negotiate rates, communicate with brokers/shippers on Carrier’s behalf, and relay Rate Confirmations. Dispatcher does not guarantee any number, lane, or rate of Loads; Carrier need not accept any Load.
3. Relationship. Dispatcher is an independent contractor, NOT a licensed broker or freight forwarder, takes no title to freight, and is not liable for cargo loss, damage, delay, or claims. Carrier controls its drivers, equipment, and routing.
4. Limited Authorization. Carrier authorizes Dispatcher, on a limited basis, to communicate with brokers/shippers and book Loads per Carrier’s preferences. Each booking is confirmed by Carrier or auto-booked within pre-approved limits; consent is recorded. Carrier may revoke any booking before dispatch.
5. Carrier Obligations. Carrier shall maintain active USDOT/MC authority; insurance of not less than $1,000,000 auto liability and $100,000 cargo; qualified, DOT-compliant drivers; and full FMCSA/DOT compliance (HOS, ELD, drug & alcohol). Carrier shall keep its information accurate and report any lapse.
6. Dispatch Fee & Payment. Fee: 5% of Gross Line-Haul Revenue per Load booked through Dispatcher and delivered. Example: $2,000 line-haul → $100 fee (fuel/lumper/detention excluded). Earned on booked+delivered; collected by settlement deduction or invoice per onboarding. Carrier gets an itemized statement and may audit. Fee disputes: within 15 days in writing; Rate Confirmation and LoadBoot records control. No fee on Carrier’s own loads; no double fee. Late fees may accrue 1.5%/mo.
7. Cancellations / TONU. Cancelled Loads, TONU, detention, and layover follow Dispatcher’s published policies. No fee on a cancelled Load unless a TONU/cancellation payment is received.
8. Records / System of Record. The LoadBoot platform is the authoritative record for Loads, Rate Confirmations, bookings, consents, communications, and Fees, and governs any dispute absent clear error.
9. Term & Termination. Effective on acceptance; either party may terminate on 30 days’ notice, or immediately for material breach, loss of authority, or lapse of insurance. Earned Fees survive.
10. Non-Exclusivity / Non-Circumvention. Non-exclusive unless a separate addendum is signed. For the Term + 180 days, Carrier shall not circumvent Dispatcher to avoid Fees on relationships first introduced by Dispatcher.
11. Confidentiality / Indemnity / Liability. Each party keeps the other’s non-public info confidential. Carrier indemnifies Dispatcher for claims from Carrier’s operations. Dispatcher’s liability is capped at Fees paid in the prior 3 months; no indirect/consequential damages.
12. Governing Law / Disputes. Texas law. Binding arbitration under AAA Commercial Rules in Dallas County, TX; either party may seek injunctive relief.
13. Miscellaneous. Entire agreement with Exhibit A and onboarding terms; amendments in signed writing (incl. electronic); severability; force majeure; no assignment without consent; electronic notices; ESIGN/UETA consent; counterparts.
Exhibit A — Fee Schedule. Dispatch Fee 5% of Gross Line-Haul Revenue per booked & delivered Load. $2,000 → $100. Excludes fuel surcharge, lumper, detention. No fee on cancelled Loads absent TONU. Collected via settlement deduction or invoice per onboarding.
