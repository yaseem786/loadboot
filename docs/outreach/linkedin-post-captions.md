# LinkedIn post captions — premium, keyword-optimized

## How ranking actually works on LinkedIn now (deep-research findings)

- **Hashtags are no longer a discovery tool.** LinkedIn removed hashtag-following in late 2024. Hashtags now only act as **topic signals** for the algorithm (360Brew AI). So don't rely on them — use 4-6 accurate ones, no more.
- **Keywords in the body are what rank you.** The AI reads your text to decide who sees it. Weave real search terms (truck dispatch, load board, freight broker, owner-operator, GPS tracking, double-brokering, reefer/flatbed) naturally into the copy.
- **The first 1-2 lines matter most** — that's all that shows before "see more." Put your strongest keyword hook there.
- **Comments drive reach.** End with a question. A post with replies gets shown to far more people.
- **Google also indexes LinkedIn company posts** — so keyword-rich copy helps outside LinkedIn too.
