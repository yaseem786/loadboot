- **Carrier stays anonymous** — no name, no MC/DOT, no factor name. Brokers can't reverse-search FMCSA/SAFER to reach the carrier directly; sourcing and booking run through LoadBoot (this is how DAT-style carrier boards protect the marketplace).
- **Primary keywords woven in:** box truck capacity, box truck fleet, box truck freight, verified carrier, FMCSA, carrier capacity, Philadelphia / Delaware Valley / Northeast, moving & relocation freight, short-haul / final-mile, factoring / NOA, live GPS tracking, rate confirmation, carrier packet.
- **Box truck = a niche the big fleets skip** (shorter-haul, less-regulated, premium/faster turnaround) — matches how brokers actually search for this equipment.
- **Northeast capacity is tightening in 2026** (spot rates up YoY, driver availability down) — the "capacity is thin" hook is timely and true.
- **First 1-2 lines carry the strongest keyword hook** (all that shows before "see more" on LinkedIn); the post ends with a **question** to drive comments (reach).
- Hashtags are topic signals only now — 5-6 accurate ones, no more.
