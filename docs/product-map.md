# LoadBoot Product Map — har portal, har tab, kya aur kyun
Walkthrough guide: is naqshe ke sath tab-by-tab screenshots review karein.

## Bara khaka (business model se mapping)
- **Website (loadboot.com)** = dukan ka darwaza — carrier/broker/shipper ko lata hai
- **Carrier Portal** = customer ka roz ka tool (jo 5% deta hai)
- **Partner Portal** = broker/shipper/facility ka tool (jo loads lata hai)
- **Command Center (CC)** = AAP ka cockpit — dispatch service yahan se chalti hai
- **Developer Portal** = future API/integrations

## Command Center — 64 tabs, 10 groups (staff = aap)
**Overview:** Dashboard (din ka snapshot) · Ops Radar (kya garbar hai abhi) · Management · BI · Analytics Control Center (GA4 — keys pending) · Business Analytics
**Operations (dil yahan hai):** Dispatch board · Booking requests (carrier→load approve; SOP §12 checks) · Carriers · Contacts · Loads & trips · Smart matching · Carrier Scorecards · Dispatch & trips · Live map (GPS trails) · Fleet & drivers · License/medical expiry · Documents · Onboarding & compliance · Verification Center · POD Review (driver ki upload approve/reject) · Exceptions · Load Intake · Trip Control Tower · Workflow Builder · Automation
**Sales & CRM:** CRM & leads · Forms inbox (website forms yahan girti hain) · Brokers & shippers · Partner intake · Broker SLA
**Support:** Tickets (carrier ki "raise issue" yahan aati hai)
**SEO & Website:** SEO & redirects · Reports
**Communications:** Messages · Notifications · Delivery health (email/SMS ki sehat) · Announcements (carriers ko broadcast) · Team chat
**Finance:** Invoices & settlements (aap ka 5% yahan) · Finance analytics · Referral Program
**Marketing:** Content & posts · Brand Kit · Form/Email Builder · Template Studio · Audiences · Campaign Manager · Marketing Intelligence · Campaigns
**Administration:** Staff & roles · Automations · Audit log · Module registry · System health · Integrations · Webhooks · Plugins · Feature flags · Settings

## Carrier Portal — 9 tabs + settings
Dashboard (KPIs, banners, aaj ka trip) · Rating (inDrive-style + account health) · Available loads (Post-a-Truck, filters, profit chips, AI best) · My trips (confirm/start/navigate/GPS/stamps/POD/emergency/reloads/rate) · Fleet (drivers/trucks + maintenance) · Finance (invoices, disputes, P&L, expenses, IFTA) · Documents (+Scan to PDF) · Support · Account · Settings (dark mode, cost/mile, push, availability)

## Partner Portal — role se shakal badalti hai
**Broker:** post loads → booking requests approve (trust profile dekh kar) → docs checklist → shipment inbox → invoices → ratings → referral → account
**Shipper:** request freight → shipment cards (stepper) → invoices → account
**Facility:** dock appointments (week strip, check-in/complete) → invoices

## Review ka tareeqa (jab aap SS bhejein)
Har tab pe 4 sawal: (1) Ye kis business sawal ka jawab hai? (2) Customer/aap iske baghair kya khota? (3) Is mn kya kami/bhetri? (4) Kya ye tab zaroori hai ya merge/remove ho? — Mera bhi yehi framework hoga.
