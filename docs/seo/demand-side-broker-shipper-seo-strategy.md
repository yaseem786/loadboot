# Demand-side SEO — bringing brokers & shippers in organically

**Goal:** carriers already arrive via search. Brokers and shippers don't, because they aren't searching the same way. This plan targets the searches they *do* make, and turns each into a page.

---

## 1. Why carriers come and the demand side doesn't (the core insight)

A carrier with an empty truck is in pain **today** and searches with high intent: "find loads for my truck", "truck dispatch service". That traffic exists and you already capture it.

Brokers and shippers are not in that same acute pain:

- **Brokers** already have DAT/Truckstop. They only search when the pain flares: **the bill.** DAT runs ~$195–$345/month and renewals jump 25–45%, and brokers openly complain about cost and fake loads (19,000 fake loads posted under one broker's name, March 2025). *(sources below)*
- **Shippers** don't search "load board" at all. They search their problem: **"how do I ship this cheaper / without a broker taking a cut / without getting scammed."**

So you don't win the demand side with product pages. You win it by **ranking on their pain**, then showing that "free + software + verified carriers" solves it. That's the whole strategy.

**Your unfair advantage in the copy:** DAT and Truckstop *charge* brokers and shippers. **You don't.** Every one of these pages leads with free, because that's the one thing the incumbents can't say.

**Broker page to build:** `free-load-board-for-brokers.html`
Target head term: *free load board for brokers / DAT alternative*.
Problem-first sections: (1) what DAT actually costs you and why, (2) the fake-load problem and how our verified board kills it, (3) free posting + the software DAT makes you pay extra for (GPS, invoicing, POD), (4) double-brokering protection, (5) "who can post" honesty (licensed brokers), (6) FAQ schema on all the cost/fraud queries, (7) CTA → create-broker-account.

## 3. Shipper keyword map (their pain = broker margin + fraud + no visibility)

Shippers almost never search "load board." They search the problem:

| Search query | Intent | Our angle |
|---|---|---|

| ship direct to carrier / direct shipper load board | high | You keep the 15–20% broker margin |
| post a load direct to carrier | high | Free posting, no middleman |
| cheapest way to ship a full truckload | cost | Cut the broker markup, pay the carrier direct |
| how to find reliable carriers | fear | Every carrier FMCSA + insurance verified, continuously |
| how to vet a carrier / avoid freight fraud | fear | We do the vetting for you, on every load |
| how to avoid double brokering (shipper) | fear | Verified carriers only + GPS proof of the real truck |
| freight tracking for shippers | visibility | Live GPS + geofenced arrive/depart on every load |

**Shipper page to build:** `ship-direct-to-carrier.html` (or `direct-shipper-load-board.html`)
Problem-first sections: (1) what the broker margin actually costs you (15–20%), (2) but going direct means *you* have to vet carriers — here's the catch, (3) how we vet every carrier so you don't have to (FMCSA, insurance, health, continuous), (4) free posting + live GPS + dock proof + you pay the carrier direct, (5) fraud/double-brokering protection, (6) "moves under licensed brokerage where required" honesty guard, (7) FAQ schema, (8) CTA → create-shipper-account.

---

## 4. The two-sided reframe you asked for

You're right that this isn't a dispatcher play — it's a marketplace. The messaging shift across both pages:

- **Old frame:** "we dispatch, we handle the broker for you"
- **New frame:** "post your freight here free — verified carriers accept, offer, or counter; you track it, invoice it, pay it, all in one app"

That's the DAT/Truckstop model **minus the fee**, plus verification the incumbents don't do well. If brokers and shippers post from all 48 states, carriers never lack freight and you stop sourcing manually. That vision is exactly what these pages should sell — freight-supply as the product, not dispatch labor.

---

## 5. Cross-linking (so the new pages rank and feed each other)

- Add both to `NAV_MENU` under a "Post freight" grouping (or Solutions)
- Add both to the footer Partners column
- Link `brokers.html` → `free-load-board-for-brokers.html` and `shipper-solutions.html` → `ship-direct-to-carrier.html` (money page below the product page)
- Add both to `sitemap.xml` and `llms.txt`
- Internal links from `market-rates.html`, `load-board.html`, `ghost-loads-load-board-problems.html`

**No duplicate content:** these are *problem/search* pages (DAT cost, broker margin, fraud). `brokers.html` and `shipper-solutions.html` stay as *product* pages. Different keywords, different intent, they link to each other — no cannibalization.

---

## 6. Honesty guards (must not break)

- LoadBoot is not a freight broker — where broker authority is legally required, freight moves through licensed partners. Every shipper page keeps this line.
- "Free for brokers and shippers" is true and published — safe to lead with.
- Don't claim DAT's exact current price as fact on the page (it changes); say "typically $195–$345/mo" or link out. Verify before publishing.
