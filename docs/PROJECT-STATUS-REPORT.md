## 1. WHAT LOADBOOT IS
A US truck-dispatch platform connecting SHIPPERS → licensed BROKERS → CARRIERS with LoadBoot's Command
Center (staff ops) in the middle. Revenue: flat 5% dispatch fee charged to carriers (pricing may evolve).
Target scale: hundreds of millions of accounts. Core principle: **100% automation of operations, with
humans kept ONLY at money movement, verification/violations, and legal sign-off.** Second principle:
**no fake data anywhere** — every number on every screen is computed from real rows and states its basis.

## 3. THE AUTOMATED VALUE CHAIN (all live, all matrix-proven)
1. **Shipper posts a Load Tender** (`cc_shipper_post_load`) — SOP §4 REQUIRED fields enforced (ref/PO,
   both contacts, commodity/weight/pieces, equipment, cargo value, facility notes, dock hours); hazmat
   info is a LEGAL hard-stop. Incomplete tenders are refused with missing fields NAMED.
2. **Open broker pool** — every active licensed broker sees the full decision detail instantly;
   first-come CLAIM (concurrency-safe) or Command-Center assignment.
3. **Broker quotes → TENDERS to dispatch** (`cc_broker_tender_shipment`) — becomes a partner_load through
   the proven submit path; the **ready-gate** (DB trigger) FORCES the complete industry rate card
   (detention $/hr + free hrs, layover, TONU, lumper policy) + scheduling (FCFS/appointment/window).
   Same gate on Command-Center direct posts. Rate standards live in a versioned, staff-editable library
   (`rate_standards`); every post auto-records a RATE AGREEMENT (who, when, snapshot, standards version).
4. **Matching engine** (`cc_match_eligibility`/`cc_match_rank`) — hard eligibility then explainable
   scoring (score == sum of shown factors). **AUTO-DISPATCH** (`cc_auto_dispatch_run`, service-role,
   flag `auto_dispatch` staging ON / prod OFF): every ready load auto-offered to top-ranked carriers,
   RE-READING each carrier's LIVE dispatch preferences (min $/mi, equipment, lanes) AND listed services
   (hazmat/team/liftgate/...) on every run — a pref/service violation is never auto-offered.
5. **Carrier accepts** (recorded written approval) → transactional booking (stale-version guard, one
   winner, others expired, trip + checklist created) → **RATE CONFIRMATION auto-issues** (immutable
   18-item snapshot, no update path exists, carrier acknowledges once) → **30-item DISPATCH SHEET**
   assembled live (portal + Pocket).
6. **In transit:** consent-first tracking, arrive/depart detention stamps (measured minutes), 🚨
   proof-required emergency flow, exception center, Trip Control Tower.
7. **Delivery:** POD upload → review → **DELIVERY DOC PACK** (`cc_delivery_doc_pack`: REQUIRED
   POD/RC/invoice, CONDITIONAL dwell stamps + receipts, packet_ready flag, 3-year retention rule) →
   invoice prep → finance lifecycle (receivables/payables/reconcile; maker-checker on settlements).
8. **Referrals 100% automated:** ?ref link auto-captured on the website → auto-claimed at first carrier
   portal entry → commissions AUTO-ACCRUE via DB trigger the moment a fee invoice is sent/paid (5-level
   chain, 15-day hold, auto-promote) → referrer requests payout with bank details → the ONLY human gate:
   staff approve/paid (money moves on the normal rail). Affiliates (influencers) get a full dashboard
   without needing a carrier org.
9. **Payments rail:** per-org bank profiles (self-managed, masked last-4 even to owner, staff-verified,
   any edit resets verification). NO money ever moves automatically anywhere in the platform.
10. **Governance:** Amazon-style ACCOUNT HEALTH for every account kind (carrier/broker/shipper) —
    0-100, score == 100 − itemized deductions, role-specific industry duties, staff violations ledger
    (warning −5/violation −15/critical −40, written reason required, instant notification), CC board
    worst-first with inline issue/resolve. PRE-BOOK GATE (`cc_prebook_check`) = consolidated go/no-go
    over SOP §12/§15 conditions.
