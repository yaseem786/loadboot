**App name:** LoadBoot — Trucking OS
**Short description (80 chars max):**
"Loads, trips, GPS proof, PODs & money tools for owner-operators and fleets."
**Full description:**
LoadBoot is the operating system for trucking. Built for owner-operators, small fleets, brokers and shippers.
• Post your truck — matching loads find you (equipment, dates, rate, radius)
• See estimated profit on every load using YOUR cost per mile
• Run trips: navigation, arrive/depart timestamps, detention minutes, live GPS sharing
• Upload PODs and scan documents to PDF — even offline
• Track invoices, expenses, IFTA state miles and truck maintenance
• Trip-verified ratings between carriers, brokers and shippers
• Flat 5% dispatch service — no contracts, no monthly minimums
Free during beta. Your documents stay private and are never sold.
**Category:** Business · **Tags:** trucking, freight, dispatch, owner operator
